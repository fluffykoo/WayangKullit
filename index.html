About Us

Preserving the Art of Wayang Kulit

Our theater celebrates the rich cultural tradition of shadow puppetry. With intricate puppets and talented puppet masters (dalangs), our performances bring timeless stories to life.

Our dalangs have mastered the ancient art of storytelling with shadows.
